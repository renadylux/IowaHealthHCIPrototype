About Us

Who are we?

We are students in Kyle Rectors Human Computer Interaction (HCI) class at University of Iowa based out of Iowa City. Your designers are looking to further thier knowledge in HCI by designing this application. They go by code names Ry-dawg, Just-up, Rain shine, and the Jester.

Mission Statment

It is our mission at Iowa Health to improve student life through mood tracking. With so many challenges students face in college, mood, stress and mental health can be an issue. Through this application, we to aid students in lowering stress and staying happy ☺
